lemon

This article is about the fruit. For other uses, see Lemon (disambiguation). "Lemons" redirects here. For the surname, see Lemons (surname). Not to be confused with Iemon.

The lemon (Citrus limon) is a species of small evergreen trees in the flowering plant family Rutaceae, native to Asia, primarily Northeast India (Assam), Northern Myanmar or China.[2] The tree's ellipsoidal yellow fruit is used for culinary and non-culinary purposes throughout the world, primarily for its juice, which has both culinary and cleaning uses.
